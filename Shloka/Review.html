The 'Cirque du Soleil' is back in town and is more extraordinary then ever!

By Shloka Gohil
Published February 22nd @ 2:22pm

Cirque du Soleil Performance

Just from the first ten minutes, one can tell that this was going to be an extraordinary experience.

Imagine trying to make your audience laugh, cry, and wonder just through the use of costumes and acrobatics. It may sound difficult but, for the Cirque du Soleil, it's a piece of cake. This performance was truly an extravagant experience for people of all different ages. All the performers put their entire heart and soul into the act, creating a memorable experience for their audience. The show was a bit chaotic in the beginning as a lot of action was happening at once but after that, eyes were wide open all throughout.

From the perspective of the audience who really enjoyed the show, the stage was showing a lot of pure emotion. Whether it was through the costumes, makeup, facial expressions, or just the acting in general. One moment the audience was laughing at a man pretending to be different animals, then they were wowing the talented acrobatic fish, and after that they were just mesmerized by the boat and ocean portion of the show. The audience was constantly amazed by what had to come next.

Although the overall theme of the show was hard to understand, one part of the show that really stood out was when the performers used the pool as an ocean and a floating platform as an island. Some were synchronized swimming while others were performing dangerous acrobatics on a moving platform. Even though both groups were staging different moves, they were still somehow in perfect sync with the music as well as each other. There was also a scene with a hanging boat prop where men and women performed flying acrobatics. The synchronization here was the most impressive and interesting as they were moving the prop, while performing. The trust the men and women had for each other was crucial during this part of the show.

Anyone can agree that the stage crew and make-up artists worked just as hard as the performers. Without all the beautiful makeup and costumes, the performance wouldn't have been as lavish. The lighting always matched the mood of whatever scene was happening on stage so, the entire atmosphere can be filled with the same emotion of either joy, fear, or sadness. The most impressive part was how the soundtrack perfectly corresponded with the actions of the performers. If they were to do something funny, then there would be a silly sound effect.

It is truly amazing how many talented minds and performers it must have taken to put together something as extraordinary as the Cirque du Soleil. This is most definitely a show worth spending the money for as it is recommended by many people such as myself. My rating would most definitely be a solid nine out of ten.
